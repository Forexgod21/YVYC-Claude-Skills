# How To Use: Brand Visual Identity Builder

**Category:** Design
**Skill File:** `SKILL.md`
**Original Source:** Alex AI Updates (public Facebook post, 2026)

---

## What This Skill Does

Turns Claude into a brand design expert that builds you a complete, specific visual
identity system. Not vague direction — exact deliverables. Real hex codes, real font
names, real logo direction, and the visual rules that keep your brand consistent
across every piece of content you create.

---

## When To Use It

- You're launching a new brand and need a visual identity from scratch
- Your brand looks inconsistent and you need defined rules
- You want to hand a designer a clear brief instead of a Pinterest board
- Your brand blends in and you need to stand out visually
- You're rebranding and need a fresh identity system

---

## How To Activate It

Once installed, activate this skill by saying something like:

> "Build a complete visual identity system for [brand name] in [niche] targeting [audience]."

Other ways to trigger it:
- "What hex codes and fonts should [brand] use?"
- "Create a visual identity for [brand name] that stands out from [competitors]"
- "I need a brand identity system I can hand to a designer"

---

## What To Provide

| Input | Required? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Brand name | Yes | "YVYC" |
| Niche or industry | Yes | "AI tools and creative ecosystems" |
| Target audience | Yes | "Builders, creators, and entrepreneurs" |
| Brand personality | No | "Bold, intelligent, innovative, human" |
| Competitors to avoid | No | "Nothing that looks like generic SaaS" |
| Existing elements | No | "I already use gold as a primary color" |

---

## Example Usage

**You say:**
> "Build a complete visual identity system for YVYC in the AI tools and creative
> ecosystem space. Target audience is builders, creators, and entrepreneurs.
> Brand personality is bold, intelligent, innovative, and human. Nothing that
> looks like generic SaaS. I already use gold as a primary color."

**Claude delivers:**
> A complete identity system with exact hex codes for a full color palette built
> around gold, specific font pairings with usage rules, logo direction brief,
> visual do's and don'ts, and the one signature decision that makes YVYC
> impossible to confuse with any other AI tools brand.

---

## Pro Tips

- Run Skill #1 (Complete Design Strategy Blueprint) first — this skill goes deeper on the specifics
- The more you tell Claude about competitors to differentiate from, the sharper the output
- If you already have colors or fonts you love, mention them — Claude builds around what you have, not over it
- Save the output as your brand bible — reference it every time you create visual content
