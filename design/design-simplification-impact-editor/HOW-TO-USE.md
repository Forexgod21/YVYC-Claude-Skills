# How To Use: Design Simplification & Impact Editor

**Category:** Design
**Skill File:** `SKILL.md`
**Original Source:** Alex AI Updates (public Facebook post, 2026)

---

## What This Skill Does

Turns Claude into a ruthless design editor that takes your existing design brief
and optimizes it for maximum visual impact. It cuts everything that doesn't belong,
sharpens everything that's weak, fills in what's missing, and hands you back a
brief that's tighter, cleaner, and more powerful than what you started with.
Then it tells you exactly what it changed and why.

---

## When To Use It

- You've already written a design brief but something feels off
- Your brief is too long, too vague, or too complicated
- You want a professional second opinion before handing it to a designer
- Your design direction has too many elements pulling in different directions
- You want to know what's weakening your current visual decisions

---

## How To Activate It

Once installed, activate this skill by saying something like:

> "Review and optimize this design brief for maximum impact: [paste your brief]"

Other ways to trigger it:
- "Cut the fat from this design direction and make it stronger: [paste brief]"
- "What's wrong with this design brief and how do I fix it: [paste brief]"
- "Ruthlessly edit this for visual impact: [paste brief]"

---

## What To Provide

| Input | Required? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Existing design brief | Yes | Paste the full brief directly into the message |
| Design goal | No | "Drive clicks to the Luna Universe book page" |
| Target audience | No | "Parents of children ages 3-8" |
| Platform or format | No | "Instagram carousel" |

---

## Example Usage

**You say:**
> "Review and optimize this design brief for maximum impact. Goal is to drive
> book sales. Audience is parents of kids ages 3-8. Format is Instagram carousel.
>
> Brief: Use warm colors that feel friendly and inviting. Include the book cover
> somewhere. Make sure the text is readable. Use our brand fonts. Keep it clean
> and not too busy. Show the character Luna. Make parents feel like this book
> is special and worth buying. Include a call to action at the end."

**Claude delivers:**
> An optimized brief that's half the length and twice as powerful — with every
> vague instruction replaced by a specific decision, every weak suggestion
> converted into a rule, and a clear edit summary explaining exactly what was
> cut, what was sharpened, and what was missing from the original.

---

## Pro Tips

- Don't clean up your brief before pasting — give Claude the raw version so it can do its job
- The Edit Summary section is where the real learning happens — read it carefully
- Run this skill on outputs from any of the other design skills to tighten them further
- This skill is the quality control checkpoint for your entire design process

---

## Installation

1. Copy the `SKILL.md` file from this folder
2. Place it in your Claude skills directory
3. Restart Claude if needed
4. You're ready to go
