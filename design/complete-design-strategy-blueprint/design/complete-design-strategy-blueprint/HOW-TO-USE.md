# How To Use: Complete Design Strategy Blueprint

**Category:** Design
**Skill File:** `SKILL.md`
**Original Source:** Alex AI Updates (public Facebook post, 2026)

---

## What This Skill Does

Turns Claude into a professional creative director who builds you a complete visual
design strategy from scratch. You give it your brand name, niche, and audience —
it gives you a full blueprint covering color psychology, typography, design flow,
and the exact decisions that make your brand impossible to confuse with anyone else.

---

## When To Use It

- You're starting a new brand and need a visual foundation
- Your content looks too generic and you can't figure out why
- You need a design plan before you create any assets
- You want every visual decision to be intentional, not accidental

---

## How To Activate It

Once installed, activate this skill by saying something like:

> "Create a complete design strategy blueprint for [your brand] in the [niche] space targeting [audience]."

Other ways to trigger it:
- "Build me a visual design strategy for [brand/topic]"
- "I need a design plan that makes my content stop looking like everyone else"
- "Give me a brand visual direction for [project]"

---

## What To Provide

| Input | Required? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Brand or topic name | Yes | "Luna Universe" |
| Niche or industry | Yes | "Children's books and education" |
| Target audience | Yes | "Parents of children ages 3-8" |
| Current design problems | No | "Everything looks too corporate" |
| Competitor references | No | "Different from Disney's color-heavy style" |

---

## Example Usage

**You say:**
> "Create a complete design strategy blueprint for Luna Universe in the children's
> book and education niche, targeting parents of kids ages 3 to 8. I want it to
> feel magical but not cartoonish, and nothing like Disney."

**Claude delivers:**
> A full blueprint with visual objective, audience profile, color palette with hex
> codes, typography pairings, design flow principles, and the signature design
> decisions that make Luna Universe visually ownable.

---

## Pro Tips

- The more specific your audience description, the sharper the output
- Use this FIRST before any other design skill — it's the foundation everything else builds on
- If you already have brand colors you love, mention them — Claude will build around them

---

## Installation

1. Copy the `SKILL.md` file from this folder
2. Place it in your Claude skills directory
3. Restart Claude if needed
4. You're ready to go
