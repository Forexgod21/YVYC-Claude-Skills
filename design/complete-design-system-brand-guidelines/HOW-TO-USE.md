# How To Use: Complete Design System & Brand Guidelines

**Category:** Design
**Skill File:** `SKILL.md`
**Original Source:** Alex AI Updates (public Facebook post, 2026)

---

## What This Skill Does

Turns Claude into a brand guidelines expert that builds you a complete, documented
design system from scratch. Full color architecture with hex codes, typography rules
for every use case, spacing and layout principles, visual tone of voice, a concrete
example library, and a quick-reference one-pager. Everything a designer, contractor,
or team member needs to stay on-brand without asking you a single question.

---

## When To Use It

- You're ready to document your brand visuals into one authoritative reference
- You're bringing on designers or team members and need to brief them properly
- Your brand looks different everywhere it appears and you need to fix that permanently
- You want every future design decision to be fast, consistent, and effortless
- You're building a brand that needs to scale

---

## How To Activate It

Once installed, activate this skill by saying something like:

> "Build a complete design system and brand guidelines document for [brand name]."

Other ways to trigger it:
- "Create a brand bible for [brand] that makes every future design decision effortless"
- "Document my brand visuals into a complete style guide for [brand name]"
- "Build brand guidelines for [brand] that I can hand to any designer"

---

## What To Provide

| Input | Required? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Brand name | Yes | "YVYC — Your Vision Your Creation" |
| Existing brand elements | No | "Gold, black, deep purple. Cosmic aesthetic." |
| Brand personality | No | "Bold, intelligent, innovative, human, expansive" |
| Use cases | No | "Social media, website, presentations, merchandise" |
| Team size | No | "Small team of 3-5 collaborators" |

---

## Example Usage

**You say:**
> "Build a complete design system and brand guidelines document for YVYC —
> Your Vision Your Creation. Existing elements: gold as primary, black and
> deep purple supporting. Cosmic aesthetic. Brand personality: bold,
> intelligent, innovative, human, expansive. Use cases: social media,
> website, presentations, and merchandise. Small team of 3-5 collaborators
> will use this."

**Claude delivers:**
> A complete YVYC brand guidelines document covering: full color system with
> primary/secondary/accent/neutral palettes and all hex codes, typography
> architecture for all use cases, spacing and layout principles, visual tone
> of voice rules, example library with correct and incorrect applications,
> and a quick-reference one-pager the team can keep open while working.

## Pro Tips

- Treat it as a living document — update it every time a brand decision is made
- The Quick Reference Summary is what your team will actually use day-to-day — make sure it's the first thing they see
