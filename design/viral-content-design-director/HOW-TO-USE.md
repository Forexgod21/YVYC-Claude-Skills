# How To Use: Viral Content Design Director

**Category:** Design
**Skill File:** `SKILL.md`
**Original Source:** Alex AI Updates (public Facebook post, 2026)

---

## What This Skill Does

Turns Claude into a viral content design director that gives you complete visual
direction for up to 20 content pieces in one shot. For every single piece you get
the layout, visual hierarchy, color treatment, typography placement, and — most
importantly — the one design decision that stops the scroll in under 2 seconds.
This is batch content planning with precision, not guesswork.

---

## When To Use It

- You're planning a content batch and want every piece to be intentional
- You're tired of posting content that gets ignored
- You need to brief a designer or use an AI image tool and need clear direction
- You want to understand what makes each piece of your content visually shareable
- You're building a content calendar and want visual variety with brand consistency

---

## How To Activate It

Once installed, activate this skill by saying something like:

> "Create complete design direction for 10 content pieces for [niche] on [platform]."

Other ways to trigger it:
- "Give me scroll-stopping design direction for 20 [niche] posts on [platform]"
- "Direct the visual design for my next content batch on [platform]"
- "What should each of these content pieces look like visually?"

---

## What To Provide

| Input | Required? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Niche or topic | Yes | "Children's books and parenting" |
| Number of pieces | Yes | "10 pieces" |
| Platform | Yes | "Instagram" |
| Brand identity | No | "Gold, black, deep purple. Clean and cosmic." |
| Content topics | No | List of specific subjects for each piece |

---

## Example Usage

**You say:**
> "Create complete design direction for 10 Instagram content pieces for Luna
> Universe in the children's books and parenting niche. Brand colors are gold,
> black, and deep purple. Topics: 1) Why bedtime stories matter, 2) How Luna
> faces her fears, 3) 5 ways to make reading fun, 4) Behind the book creation,
> 5) Parent testimonial highlight, 6) Character introduction, 7) Reading tip
> carousel, 8) Book giveaway announcement, 9) Author story, 10) Call to order."

**Claude delivers:**
> Complete design direction for all 10 pieces — each with layout, visual
> hierarchy sequence, color treatment, typography placement, and the specific
> scroll-stop decision. Plus a pattern summary showing the 3 design signatures
> that run through the highest-impact pieces.

---

## Pro Tips

- The scroll-stop decision for each piece is pure gold — save them and use them as your creative brief
- Providing your own content topics gets sharper direction than letting Claude generate them
- Run this skill once per content batch — don't try to do a month all at once
- Pair with Skill #3 (Social Media Design System) so the direction stays on-brand

---

## Installation

1. Copy the `SKILL.md` file from this folder

4. You're ready to go
