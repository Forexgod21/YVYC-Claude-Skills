# How To Use: Social Media Design System

**Category:** Design
**Skill File:** `SKILL.md`
**Original Source:** Alex AI Updates (public Facebook post, 2026)

---

## What This Skill Does

Turns Claude into a social media design strategist that builds you a complete,
platform-specific visual system. Not just "use your brand colors" — actual
structure. Post templates, story formats, carousel frameworks, color usage rules,
and the non-negotiable cohesion rules that make your entire feed look like it
was designed by a professional agency.

---

## When To Use It

- You're building a social media presence from scratch and need visual structure
- Your content looks random and inconsistent across posts
- You want every post to look like it belongs to the same brand without extra effort
- You're expanding to a new platform and need platform-specific guidelines
- You're handing your content process to a team member and need documented rules

---

## How To Activate It

Once installed, activate this skill by saying something like:

> "Design a complete social media visual system for [platform] in the [niche] space."

Other ways to trigger it:
- "Create visual templates and guidelines for my [platform] account"
- "Make my [platform] content look consistent and professional"
- "Build a social media design system for [brand] on [platform]"

---

## What To Provide

| Input | Required? | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Platform | Yes | "Instagram" |
| Niche or topic | Yes | "Children's books and education" |
| Brand identity | No | "Gold, black, and deep purple. Clean and cosmic." |
| Content types | No | "Feed posts, stories, and carousels" |
| Account goal | No | "Build authority and drive book sales" |

---

## Example Usage

**You say:**
> "Design a complete social media visual system for Instagram in the children's
> book and education niche. Brand colors are gold, black, and deep purple with
> a clean, cosmic feel. Content types are feed posts, stories, and carousels.
> Goal is to build authority and drive Luna Universe book sales."

**Claude delivers:**
> A full Instagram visual system covering post template structure, story layout
> rules, carousel framework from cover to CTA slide, exact color assignments
> per content type, typography size guidelines, and the 5 cohesion rules that
> make every post instantly recognizable as Luna Universe — no logo required.

---

## Pro Tips

- Run this skill once per platform — Instagram and LinkedIn have completely different visual logic
- The cohesion rules section is the most valuable output — save those and post them somewhere visible
- If you don't have a brand identity yet, run Skill #2 (Brand Visual Identity Builder) first
- Share the output document with anyone helping you create content — it becomes your style guide
